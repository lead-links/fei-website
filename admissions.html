Where do I begin?

Start with a
conversation.

Before you apply, we recommend meeting with one of our Career Education Advisors. They will help you explore your interests, goals, and aspirations, and find the program that fits your life.

When you are well informed, you will feel confident about your choice. That is the whole point.

How it works

Four simple steps.

No application deadlines. No application fees. Classes start every three to four weeks, depending on your program.

  1. 1

    Reach out

    Call us or request information online. We will answer your questions honestly.

  2. 2

    Meet an advisor

    Sit down with a Career Education Advisor to explore programs and your goals.

  3. 3

    Plan & apply

    Review your financial aid options and complete your application, no fee, no deadline.

  4. 4

    Start class

    New classes begin every 3-4 weeks, so you can start soon after enrolling.
