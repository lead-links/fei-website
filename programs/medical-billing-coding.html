What does a Medical Billing & Coding specialist do?

The behind-the-scenes
work healthcare runs on.

Medical Billing and Coding specialists translate patient visits into accurate codes and clean claims, the paperwork that gets providers paid and keeps patient records straight. If you are detail-oriented, comfortable at a computer, and want a healthcare career that does not require clinical work, this program is built for you.

Curriculum

What you will master

  • Applied Medical Terminology
  • Chart Review for Coding Accuracy
  • ICD-10-CM, CPT, and HCPCS Coding
  • Insurance Claims Processing and Reimbursement
  • Anatomy, Physiology, and Human Disease
  • Combined Billing and Coding Training

Careers

Where this
can take you

  • Medical Billing Specialist
  • Medical Coder
  • Insurance Claims Processor
  • Health Information Clerk

Billing and coding roles are in steady demand across Miami-Dade clinics, hospitals, and insurance offices, many of them fully remote, ask your advisor about current local hiring trends.
