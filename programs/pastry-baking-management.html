What does this program prepare you for?

Part craft, part
leadership, all pastry.

Pastry & Baking Management goes beyond the bench, you will master the techniques behind breads, cakes, chocolate, and desserts, then build the leadership, teamwork, and business sense it takes to run a pastry program or bakery. If you want a creative, hands-on career in food with a real path into management, this program is built for you.

Curriculum

What you will master

  • Fundamental Pastry and Baking Techniques
  • Artisan Breads
  • Cakes, Fillings, and Desserts
  • Chocolate, Sugar Arts, and Confections
  • International and Modern Desserts
  • Dessert Decorating and Presentation
  • Food Safety, Sanitation, and Kitchen Rules
  • Nutrition, Customer Service, and Hospitality
  • Leadership, Teamwork, and Professional Ethics

Careers

Where this
can take you

  • Pastry Shop Supervisor
  • Bakery Manager (entry path)
  • Head Baker
  • Pastry Program Coordinator

Skilled pastry and baking talent is in steady demand across Miami-Dade bakeries, hotels, and restaurants, ask your advisor about current local hiring trends.

Format & Schedule
