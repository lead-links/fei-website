What does this degree prepare you for?

Lead teams, run
operations, build a business.

Business Administration graduates step into the roles that keep a company moving, supervising people, managing budgets, coordinating operations, and making the calls that departments depend on. If you want a well-rounded business education that covers management, marketing, finance, and the practical skills to run something of your own, this program is built for you.

Curriculum

What you will master

  • Business Principles and Practices
  • Entrepreneurship and Small Business Development
  • Management, Supervision, and Leadership
  • Human Resources and Managing People
  • Business Communication and Professional Work Ethic
  • Marketing, Sales, and Customer Loyalty
  • Accounting, Financial Markets, and Economics
  • Computer Applications and Business Technology
  • Business Law, Ethics, and Government Perspectives
