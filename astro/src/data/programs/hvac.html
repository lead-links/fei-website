What does an HVAC/R Technician do?

Keep South Florida
cool, warm, and running.

HVAC/R technicians install, maintain, and repair the air conditioning, heating, and refrigeration systems that homes and businesses cannot do without, especially here in Miami. If you like working with your hands, solving mechanical problems, and want a trade that is always in demand, this program is built for you.

Curriculum

What you will master

  • Installing, Maintaining, and Repairing Residential and Commercial HVAC/R Systems
  • Safe Practices with A/C and Refrigeration Equipment
  • Diagnosing Mechanical and Performance Issues
  • Electrical Systems, Controls, and Basic System Design
  • EPA and R-410A Certification Exam Preparation
  • Hands-On Lab Training with Industry Equipment

Careers

Where this
can take you

HVAC/R technicians are in steady demand across Miami-Dade, from residential service companies to commercial and refrigeration contractors actively hiring locally. Ask your advisor about current local hiring trends.
