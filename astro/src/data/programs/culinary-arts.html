What does a Culinary Arts graduate do?

Part technique, part
timing, all craft.

Culinary Arts graduates run the line, prep for service, and keep a kitchen moving under pressure, knife skills, technique, and food safety all working together in real time. If you want a hands-on career built around real cooking, not just recipes on paper, and you thrive in a fast-paced kitchen environment, this program is built for you.
