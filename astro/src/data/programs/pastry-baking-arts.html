What does a Pastry & Baking Arts graduate do?

Part precision, part
artistry, all craft.

Pastry and baking professionals turn flour, sugar, and butter into something people remember, artisan loaves, celebration cakes, and desserts that are as much about technique as they are about taste. If you like working with your hands, care about detail, and want a creative trade you can build a career on, this program is built for you.
