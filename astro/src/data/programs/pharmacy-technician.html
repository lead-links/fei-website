What does a Pharmacy Technician do?

Precision, care, and
a steady pace.

Pharmacy Technicians work alongside pharmacists to prepare and dispense medication safely and accurately, collecting prescription data, managing inventory, and helping patients get what they need when they need it. If you are detail-oriented, like the structure of a pharmacy setting, and want a healthcare career without years of school, this program is built for you.

Curriculum

What you will master

  • Prescription Data Collection and Processing
  • Assisting in Medication Preparation
  • Prescription Measuring, Packaging, and Labeling
  • Inventory Management and Insurance Processing
  • Pharmacy Software Systems
  • Supervised Externship Near Home
