1. Introduction

As per the Higher Education Act of 1965 (HEA), as amended by the Higher Education Opportunity Act of 2008 (HEOA), included below are the disclosures, reporting requirements, and consumer information. Florida Education Institute (FEI) provides prospective and current students, and other members of the FEI community access to this information in order so they can make informed decisions regarding FEI their education and career. FEI also provides information herein that is helpful for its staff and faculty to access in an effort to operate the school and its services in the best possible way.

You will also find here other pertinent information related to academics, programs, school rules and policies, financial aid opportunities for our students, facilities and services. Some of this information is also intended to supplement or provide additional information by way of our catalog, another institutional publication, FEI website, or on a third-party website. The FEI website also serves to notify current and prospective students regarding the availability and location of consumer information in accordance with certain state and federal laws applicable to our school.

Any questions or requests regarding this consumer information section may be obtained by contacting the Campus Director or services@fei.edu.

4. Website Accessibility

Florida Education Institute is committed to ensuring that its website is accessible to all visitors. Accordingly, FEI’s website has been developed using the principles and guidelines found in Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, and in accordance with WCAG 2.0 AA standards. Should you encounter an issue using FEI’s website, please contact any of our Student Services Coordinators at 305-444-1515 for further assistance or to report a problem.
