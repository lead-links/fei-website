Personal Attention

With support, accomplishing
goals becomes easier.

Personal attention is a core value at FEI. We are committed to understanding how our students learn best, what drives them, and how we can help ensure their success, and we deliver that attention every single day.

Every member of our team, from faculty to administration, cares deeply about student success. We always keep in mind the sacrifices our students make to attend school, and the reasons why they came to our school.

As Ralph Waldo Emerson put it, "the secret of education is respecting the pupil."

Career Education

A Fun, Active &
Motivating approach.

We take a Fun, Active, and Motivating (FAM) approach to career education, a warm, family-like environment where every student finds the support they need to reach their full potential.

  • Fun. Learning is most effective when it is genuinely enjoyable.
  • Active. Career skills are best absorbed through hands-on, practical experience.
  • Motivating. Students thrive on continuous support and encouragement along the way.

Our Approach

Student-centered,
employer-driven.

Every student learns their own way and at their own pace, with the support they need to thrive. Our curriculum is shaped by the demands of the job market, we listen to employers to keep our programs materials in line with the skills they value most.
