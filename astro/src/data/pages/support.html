Incredible Support

We are with you
the whole way.

The goal of Support Services at FEI is simple: to provide incredible support during each student journey to success. From enrollment to graduation to your first job, we want the entire process to be genuinely enjoyable.

Students have a better shot at success when they feel supported and encouraged, so we focus on each student at a very personal level, always making sure you know we are here to help you reach your goals.

Career Services

Help landing
the job, for real.

Our Career Services team empowers students and graduates with hands-on help, before and after graduation:

  • Job referrals & interview scheduling
  • Cover letter, resume & thank-you letter guidance
  • Interview preparation & job-readiness workshops
  • Targeting the right employers for your search
  • Ongoing career coaching, before and after graduation
  • Career development throughout your program

FEI cannot guarantee employment, but our team is fully committed to supporting motivated graduates.
